Our Journey

From a humble street cart to the city's favorite dining spot.

1998

The Beginning

We started with just three tables and a grandmother's recipe book. The aroma of our signature spices drew in the entire neighborhood.

2005

The Expansion

With lines wrapping around the block, we moved to our current downtown location, preserving the intimate atmosphere while tripling our kitchen capacity.

2015

Culinary Excellence

Our commitment to quality was recognized with the "Best Local Flavor" award, solidifying our place in the city's culinary map.

Today

A Modern Classic

Continuing the legacy by embracing modern tastes while never forgetting our roots. Now serving you digitally with just a tap.
